A resident’s case for parkland funding

By Bakr Al Qaraghuli, Editor • Sep 22, 2025

Princeton, Texas: City officials are considering an overnight construction schedule for US‑380 to accelerate delivery and reduce daytime gridlock.

What’s being proposed

Crews would work between 9 p.m. and 5 a.m., Monday through Saturday, with noise and lighting mitigations.

What’s next

The council will discuss the plan next week. Residents may submit comments via the city portal.
